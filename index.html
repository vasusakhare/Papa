Papa

Pappa, exactly one year ago I was with you, I wish I had known that you are not going to meet me again so that at least I could have shown my gratitude towards you. My soul is yearning to listen your voice.There's an emptiness in my heart, that'll never be filled even with a thousand smiles I put on my face. I was the luckiest to have you and the unluckiest to never be able to bid you goodbye on that day when you answered your eternal call. Keep your blessings on us Pappa. Every second how much we miss you I can't even tell you

-Sakhare Sir chi lek
